THE BEST ANIMALS IN THE WHOLE WORLD:

CATS

10 reasons to love cats:

  1. They can eat the same thing every day and not complain.
  2. They can stay home alone all day and the house will be fine.
  3. Two words: Whisker kisses.
  4. There is nothing like being woken up by sandpaper kitty kisses on your nose.
  5. The vibrating foot-warmer feature is awesome.
  6. Two more words: paw pads.
  7. They love you unconditionally … as long as you feed them.
  8. The Ancient Egyptians worshipped them. If you’re reading this, you probably still do today, too!
  9. Sometimes they get a little twitchy in their sleep, and it’s fun to think they’re dreaming of mice or chasing bugs.
  10. Perfect little pink noses.
